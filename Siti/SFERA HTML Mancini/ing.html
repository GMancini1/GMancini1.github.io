Who is Sfera Ebbasta?

Sfera Ebbasta, real name Gionata Boschetti, was born on 13 December 1992 in Sesto San Giovanni, in the province of Milan, Italy. Raised in a family of Albanian origin, he developed a strong interest in music, particularly hip-hop, from a young age. His musical career began around 2012-2013, when he started publishing his first songs on YouTube and SoundCloud. His talent quickly attracted attention in the world of Italian rap music. In 2015, he released his first official album, "XDVR", which was highly successful and helped consolidate his fame in the music scene. Sfera is known for his unique style and for being one of the pioneers of trap in Italy. He has continued to churn out hits with albums such as "Sfera Ebbasta" in 2016 and "Rockstar" in 2018, which have won numerous awards and reached high positions in the Italian music charts. In addition to his music, Sfera has become an icon of youth culture, influencing lifestyle, fashion and language. His social media presence has helped increase his popularity, with millions of followers calling for him to be a point of reference in the world of rap and trap music. However, his career has not been free from controversy. He has faced criticism and controversy related to some of his songs and the content of his lyrics, but has still managed to maintain a solid and loyal fan base. Today, Sfera Ebbasta is one of the most famous and influential rappers in Italy and continues to be a point of reference in the contemporary music scene. His music and media presence continue to leave a significant imprint on Italian pop culture.
